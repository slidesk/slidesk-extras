# Template: ThreeParts

This template follows this structure:

```
## Title of the slide .[#three-parts]

[[one]]

Left part

[[/one]]

and the rest of then content

[[two]]

Middle part

[[/two]]

[[three]]

Right part

[[/three]]

and it can continue here
```

And will render the slide like this:

```
        Title of the slide

Left part  |  Middle part  |  Right part

         and rest of content
       and it can continue here
```
